EXFIT AI – Data & Account Deletion Policy

This page explains how users of the EXFIT AI app can request deletion of their account and personal data. EXFIT AI is published on Google Play by BAZEUS / Fitness AI Coach Tracker-EXFIT .

In-App Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account directly within the EXFIT AI app:

  • Open the EXFIT AI app
  • Go to Settings > Deactivate & Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion – this will remove your account and associated data
